Updated for 2026 — a practical, no-nonsense comparison of two LinkedIn feed cleaners.

If your LinkedIn feed feels cluttered with ads, suggested posts and endless “Liked by” updates, you’ve probably searched for a LinkedIn feed cleaner.

Two names that often come up are LinkTopics and LinkOff.

This comparison focuses on what really matters:

  • how each tool approaches feed cleaning,
  • how much effort they require from the user,

Quick Summary

  • LinkOff is built around deep customization and multiple filtering options.
  • LinkTopics focuses on simplicity, consistency and removing the biggest sources of LinkedIn feed noise.

What Is LinkOff?

LinkOff is a LinkedIn feed customization extension designed to give users fine-grained control over what appears in the LinkedIn interface.

It offers multiple toggles and filtering options that allow users to manually adjust different feed elements.

This approach appeals to power users, but it also introduces complexity and requires ongoing adjustments as LinkedIn frequently changes how the feed works.

What LinkOff Does Well

LinkOff’s main strength is flexibility.

  • Many configuration options
  • Ability to tweak different feed elements
  • Appeals to users who enjoy hands-on customization

Common Downsides of Heavy Customization

When you rely on extensive customization, several issues tend to appear:

  • Filters may need frequent re-tuning.
  • Results can feel inconsistent across sessions.
  • Content may briefly appear before being hidden.
  • The experience depends heavily on how much time the user invests.

For many professionals, this quickly becomes friction instead of help.

What Is LinkTopics?

LinkTopics is a LinkedIn feed cleaner built around one idea: most users want a clean feed without thinking about it.

Instead of dozens of toggles, LinkTopics focuses on removing the most common sources of noise:

  • ads and promoted posts,
  • “Liked by” and reaction-driven posts,
  • suggested and recommended content,
  • off-topic posts that don’t match your interests.

The feed is cleaned automatically as you scroll, with no ongoing configuration required.

LinkTopics vs LinkOff: Which Fits Most Users?

LinkOff may fit you if:

  • You enjoy constant customization.
  • You like tweaking settings and filters.
  • You don’t mind investing time to maintain your setup.

LinkTopics fits you if:

  • You want a clean feed instantly.
  • You don’t have time for constant adjustments.
  • You use LinkedIn as a professional tool, not a hobby.

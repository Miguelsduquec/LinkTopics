Why LinkedIn Adblock Extensions Exist

LinkedIn ads are easy to dislike. They interrupt the scroll and feel commercial.

So naturally, ad blockers focus on removing:

  • promoted posts,
  • sponsored cards,
  • banner-style ads.

This solves a visible problem — but not the main one.

The Real Source of LinkedIn Feed Noise

Even without ads, your feed still contains:

  • posts liked or commented on by your connections,
  • suggested posts from people you don’t follow,
  • “Because you viewed…” recommendations,
  • engagement-driven or viral content.

None of these are ads.

This is why ad blockers alone cannot clean the LinkedIn feed.

Limitations of LinkedIn Adblock Alternatives

  • They focus only on sponsored content.
  • They don’t understand feed context.
  • They can’t remove “Liked by” or reaction-based posts.
  • They offer no topic-based filtering.
  • They treat LinkedIn like a static website.

The result: fewer ads, but the same mental noise.

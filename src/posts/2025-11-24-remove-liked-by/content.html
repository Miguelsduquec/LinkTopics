Why You Keep Seeing “Liked by” Posts

LinkedIn’s algorithm prioritizes engagement — not relevance. When someone in your network reacts to a post:

  • LinkedIn assumes you may want to see it (even if you don’t).
  • It boosts the platform’s engagement metrics.
  • It fills your feed with content you never followed.

And here’s the key problem: you cannot disable them through LinkedIn settings.

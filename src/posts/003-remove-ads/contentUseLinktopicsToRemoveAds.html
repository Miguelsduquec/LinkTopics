Frequently Asked Questions

What does Linktopics do on LinkedIn?

Linktopics helps you clean up your LinkedIn feed. It removes ads, hides likes and job posts, and lets you focus on topics you care about. This makes it easier to see real conversations and useful content.

How does Linktopics hide unwanted posts and ads?

Linktopics uses smart filters to block ads, likes, and job posts from your feed. You can also set it up to ignore certain topics or groups forever, so you won’t see things you don’t care about.

Can I choose which topics I want to follow?

Yes, you can! Linktopics lets you pick topics using tags. You can filter your feed to show only the subjects that interest you, and even create custom views for different interests.

Will I still get notifications about posts I’ve hidden?

No, if you hide or ignore a topic, Linktopics stops sending you notifications about it. You can always check your list of ignored topics and unhide them if you change your mind.

How does Linktopics help me find better content?

Linktopics highlights high-quality discussions and skill-based posts. It helps you discover real, useful content without the clutter of ads and spam, so you can focus on learning and networking.

Is it easy to customize my LinkedIn experience with Linktopics?

Yes! Linktopics is made to be simple. You can bookmark favorite topics, get notifications only for what you want, and even try out special threads called megathreads to talk about certain subjects with others.

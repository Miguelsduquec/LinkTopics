What LinkTopics actually does

LinkTopics is a lightweight Chrome extension that cleans your LinkedIn feed in real time. It hides distractions such as:

  • Sponsored and Promoted posts
  • Suggested Posts and “Trending now” sections
  • “Liked by” and reaction-based posts
  • Other algorithm-driven clutter

It doesn’t modify your LinkedIn account, automate actions or send your data anywhere. It simply filters what your browser displays.

LinkTopics is especially useful if you rely on LinkedIn for work but find the feed overwhelming. It’s ideal for:

  • Content creators analysing what works without distractions

How it compares to generic ad blockers

Most “LinkedIn adblock” searches lead to generic tools like AdBlock or uBlock. These work well for banners — but struggle on LinkedIn.

Why? LinkedIn ads are native feed posts, not traditional ads.

Where LinkTopics wins

  • LinkedIn-specific pattern detection
  • No site breakage or blocked scripts
  • Extra filtering beyond ads (Suggested Posts, reactions)

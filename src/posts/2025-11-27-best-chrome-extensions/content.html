FAQ — Chrome extensions and LinkedIn

Are Chrome extensions allowed on LinkedIn?

Yes, many users rely on browser extensions to customise their LinkedIn experience. Avoid tools that automate actions in aggressive ways (for example, spamming connections/messages).

Is a linkedin adblock enough to clean my feed?

Most generic ad blockers only remove certain assets or scripts. Because LinkedIn uses native feed ads and suggested blocks, a dedicated feed cleaner like LinkTopics is usually more effective than a basic linkedin adblock approach.

Can using too many extensions slow down LinkedIn?

Yes. If you install many extensions that interact with LinkedIn, they can slow down loading and scrolling. Keep a small stack of tools you truly use.

Which extension should I start with?

If your feed feels messy, start with a feed cleaner like LinkTopics. Once your feed is under control, add other extensions for messaging, scheduling or analytics.
